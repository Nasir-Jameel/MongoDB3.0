ii: bcrypt (encryption and decryption)
(a) encryption: sab sa pehly salt set kiya jata ha (ant shant string) phir us ko Hash(hamary password ka antshant string) k sath mila diya jata ha ta k hack na ho saky .
(b) decryption(compare) : jab hum login karty hain to server ap k login karty waqt waly password  ko Hash ma badal k pehly Hash k sath compare karta ha. Agar pehly password ka Hash abhi k Hash k sath match kar gya to login ho jaey ga.
